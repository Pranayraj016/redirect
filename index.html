About

With a firm belief that only professional education has a potential to lay a strong foundation that can result in the blissful economic and social changes to the lives of millions, Rajiv Memorial Academic Welfare Society went about reshaping diligent and dedicated Indian youths in the form of global multifaceted talents through an array of Educational Institutions. Incepted in the year 1997, the group has demonstrated meteoric growth since the beginning. Today, it has become one of the most prestigious educational hub solely devoted to equip thousands of young students in the array of higher education in diversified fields i.e,. Engineering & Technology, Management, Pharmacy, Dentistry, Teacher Education, Basic Science & Library Science and Medical Science. Presently 12 institutes are thriving under the banner.

About the GL Bajaj Institute of Management

Welcome to GL Bajaj Institute of Management, Greater Noida Driven by a strong inclination towards imparting quality education, GL Bajaj Institute of Management, Greater Noida is one of the finest Educational Institute in the Greater Noida/Delhi-NCR region. Since the establishment of GL Bajaj Group, our skilled faculty share their knowledge with their students in the best possible way to find solutions to their complex issues and problems . Since its establishment in the year 1997, GL Bajaj Group has embraced quality and innovation favourable for the overall development of its students. Continuing on its path of growth, GL Bajaj Group experienced the greatest period of success in its history with 9 campuses located in NCR and Mathura. Gl Bajaj Group has been successfully building strong professionals in the field of Management, Technology, Education, Dental Science, Engineering, Pharmacy and Library Sciences. Thanks to Mr. Pankaj Agrawal, Vice Chairman, G L Bajaj Educational Institutions, whose selfless work has won laurels for the G L Bajaj Group at various platforms. G L Bajaj Institute of Management, Greater Noida is one of the prestigious Institutions under the G L Bajaj Group offering Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) and Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA). The Institute is affiliated to Chaudhary Charan Singh University (CCS), Meerut.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is GLB-Hackathon 4.0?
GLB Hackathon 4.0 is a collaborative event where tech enthusiasts come together to innovate, solve problems, and build groundbreaking solutions. It's a platform to learn, network, and showcase your coding skills in a vibrant and inspiring environment.
Is there a limit to the number of team members?
Each team can have up to 4 members.
Are there any restrictions on who can participate?
Participants must be students or professionals in the tech industry.
Is there a specific coding experience requirement?
No, prior coding experience is not necessary. If you're interested in coding and want to learn more, this is a great opportunity.
Are international participants welcome?
Absolutely! We encourage participants from all backgrounds and nationalities to join our hackathon.
Is this hackathon suitable for beginners?
Absolutely! Our mentors and volunteers are here to guide and support beginners throughout the event. You'll have a great opportunity to learn and grow while having fun.
Can I start working on my project before the hackathon begins?
No, to ensure a fair playing field for all participants, we require that all projects be developed entirely within the timeframe of the hackathon.
What essentials do I need for the hackathon?
Make sure to bring your student ID, laptop or computer, chargers, and any hardware you plan to use. Please refrain from bringing any prohibited items such as weapons, alcohol, or drugs.
Will there be internet access available at the hackathon?
We will provide a limited number of internet connections, but we recommend that you bring your own broadband or hotspot device to ensure reliable internet access.
How will teams be shortlisted?
The selection of teams will be based on the information provided in their registration forms.
Are there any age restrictions for participation?
Minors and high school students are encouraged to participate. Please note that if you are under 18, you must have parental consent to stay on campus overnight.
Haven't found your answer? Ask a Question!
If your question isn't listed here, feel free to drop us a line at glb.hackathon@glbim.ac.in.We're happy to help!
Want to get involved?
We're always looking for enthusiastic volunteers, mentors, and judges to help make GLB Hackathon a success. Let us know if you're interested by emailing us at glb.hackathon@glbim.ac.in
